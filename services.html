Planning

During the planning stage, we'll talk about what you're looking for in your photography or videography. I'll ask questions about the session type, the location, number of people, and timeframe requirements. Think of this as the "getting to know each other" stage.

[02]

Contract & Scheduling

Once we've gotten to know each other, you can put your deposit down to secure your scheduled session & we'll formalize it with a simple contract that clearly defines what my deliverables are. I will provide you with a definite day & time in the contract for the shoot as well as a set date for my finished deliverables.

[03]

Preparation

Depending on the type of photography service, we will provide any needed guidance on preparation, outfit choices, and any other minor details. This ensures that you feel confident and ready to make the most of your photo session.

Shooting Day

We'll meet up at your shoot location at the appointed time & have a blast while we're in session. After we're done shooting, you're done! I'll reach out to you with your edited files by the date that is specified in your contract, if not sooner.
