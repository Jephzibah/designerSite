About CSRA Lawn Care

CSRA Lawn Care is a detail-oriented residential landscape management service. Areas served include Augusta Richmond County & Columbia County as well as Aiken County, SC.

Core services are:

  • Regular mowing & cleanup
  • Leaf & pine straw cleanup
  • Shrub maintenance, including weed control
  • Small tree care
  • Lawn feeding & weed control
  • Irrigation services
  • Property cleanup

All service customers are on a weekly schedule unless more visits are required during specific activities.
