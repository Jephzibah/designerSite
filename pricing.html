What Do We Charge?

We try to be as upfront as possible on pricing so you know exactly what to expect before you ever pick up the phone. Below is a list of common services & starting price points.

Landscape Maintenance

Most homes in the CSRA can be serviced for routine landscape maintenance starting at:

  • Weekly: $65/visit
  • Bi-Weekly: $90/visit

Irrigation Repair

Each irrigation system is different & will have different requirements. Our irrigation services are billed at an hourly rate plus parts cost:

  • Service call: $100 for the first hour
  • $75 per additional hour

Treatments & Fertilization

Residential fertilization & chemical treatments start at $50 per application, depeding on the property owner's needs.
